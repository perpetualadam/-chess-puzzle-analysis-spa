Chess Puzzle & Analysis

How to use this app

Puzzle Mode

Goal: Find the best move (or sequence) for the side you're playing. Drag a piece to a legal square to make a move.

  • New Puzzle — Load a new random puzzle. Use the mode dropdown for Rated, Custom (by theme/rating), Puzzle Rush, Battle (vs AI), or Daily.
  • Hint — Show engine lines and arrows for the current position (requires engine running).
  • Solution — Reveal the full solution: pieces move to the final position and arrows show the line.
  • After your correct move, the opponent’s reply plays automatically. Keep solving until the puzzle is complete.

Analysis Mode

Paste PGN or pick a game from Hall of Fame. Use Load PGN to paste or type a game ID. Analyze Game runs the engine; Export saves an annotated PGN. Use the move buttons (⏮ ◀ ▶ ⏭) to step through the game.

Engine (right panel)

Start Engine / Stop — Turn the Stockfish engine on or off. The engine is used for puzzle verification, opponent moves, and analysis. Depth and MultiPV sliders control search depth and number of lines.

Settings (gear icon)

Board theme, piece set, engine depth, MultiPV, timed puzzles, and scoring. Save and reload to apply.

Mobile

Tap and drag pieces on the board. The board area is set so dragging doesn’t scroll the page. Use the menu (☰) to open the sidebar on small screens.
